About Me


Shayanne Samarasinghe is an aspiring junior developer working in Toronto, Canada. A graduate of the University of Ottawa and Sheridan College, Shayanne began his professional career in film and television 4 years ago. Shayanne has worked in television and commercials over the last four years as a production coordinator and casting director on several productions. Around the time of the 2020 pandemic, he thought about taking the down time he had and learning a new skill. Web development was always something of an interest to him so he enrolled at the Trilogy Education Coding bootcamp at the University of Toronto. Since joining he has completed several interesting programming projects and learned many aspects of front end and back end programming. Currently, he is well on his way to becoming a full stack developer and hopes to land his first job in the tech industry in the coming months.
